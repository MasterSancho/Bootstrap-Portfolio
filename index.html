React Project

Overview:
Pulse Market is an immersive online marketplace showcasing future electronic products. It offers a dynamic platform where clients can explore and purchase products, read descriptions, check out reviews, and keep track of their orders - all through a seamless user interface. With a secure sign-up process, the users can add products to their cart, fill in their addresses, choose their payment method, and follow their orders' shipping status. On the other hand, an admin interface enables product and user management, tracking orders, and updating the shipping status.

Technical Stack:
The application was built using React.js for the frontend and Node.js for the backend.
Key dependencies include react-redux, reduxjs/toolkit, react-router-dom, axios for the frontend, and express, mongoose, bcryptjs, jsonwebtoken for the backend.
PayPal integration was achieved using react-paypal-js.

Front-End: react-redux | reduxjs/toolkit | react-router-dom | axios | react-bootstrap | react-router-bootstrap | react-icons | react-toastify | react-helmet-async | paypal/react-paypal-js

Back-End: express | mongoose | bcryptjs | jsonwebtoken | cookie-parser | multer | nodemo | concurrently | dotenv | types/express
