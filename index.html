My Projects

vCardListing - Digital Business Card for Real Estate Agents (Product App) Overview:
vCardListing serves as an all-in-one platform where real estate agents can manage their profiles and showcase property listings.
It provides clients with comprehensive, up-to-date information about houses for sale or rent at their fingertips.
Designed with a user-friendly interface, vCardListing ensures easy navigation and seamless communication between clients and agents.
Its primary function is to streamline the information sharing process, facilitating more efficient and effective client engagement.

Pulse Market (Project) Overview:
Pulse Market is an immersive online marketplace showcasing future electronic products.
It offers a dynamic platform where clients can explore and purchase products, read descriptions, check out reviews, and keep track of their orders - all through a seamless user interface.
With a secure sign-up process, the users can add products to their cart, fill in their addresses, choose their payment method, and follow their orders' shipping status.
On the other hand, an admin interface enables product and user management, tracking orders, and updating the shipping status.

Calories Tracker (Project) Overview:
Calories Tracker is a straightforward, user-friendly calorie counter application designed with vanilla JavaScript.
The application is designed to help users monitor their meals and workouts effectively, providing an accurate calculation of total calories burned and consumed.
This application serves as an essential tool for anyone aiming for a healthier lifestyle or managing their nutritional intake.
